What is Mental Health

Mental Health is describe as being associated as a person's psychological and emotional well being. From Mentalhealth.gov it describes Mental Health as including our emotional, psychological, and social well being.

It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also tells us how we determine stress going on in our lives, relates to others, and makes choices.

Mental Health is important at every stage of live, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.
