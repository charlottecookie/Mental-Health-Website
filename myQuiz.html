Welcome to the Mental Health Quiz.

This quiz is here to help you get an idea if you are currently struggling with your mental health

Please answer the questions honestly and think carefully before submitting your answers.

This quiz is for personal use and can give an indication of underlying mental health issues. This should not be used as a method of self diagnosis.

On a scale of 1-10 (1 being not often, 10 being frequently) how often are you worrying?



On a scale of 1-10 (1 being not often, 10 being frequently) do you feel overwhelmed and you are struggling at the moment?



On a scale of 1-10 (1 being not often, 10 being frequently) Do you feel your mood changes often easily?



On a scale of 1-10 (1 being not often, 10 being frequently) Do your feel your relationships are being strained?



On a scale of 1-10 (1 being not often, 10 being frequently) Do you feel your energy levels are lower than usual?



On a scale of 1-10 (1 being not often, 10 being frequently)Has your diet/eating habits been affected in a negative manner?



On a scale of 1-10 (1 being not often, 10 being frequently)Do you have any sleeping issues at night?



If you have answered more often; 6 or more out of 10 in this quiz, you may chose to seek the help of a mental health professional or see your doctor. You can also refer to my helplines page for more information
